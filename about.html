About us

Dondor Foreign Employment Agency

Provider of both skilled and unskilled labor workforce

Welcome to Dondor, your reliable partner in Recruitment and Workforce Connection solutions. Dondor Foreign Employment Agency operates as a certified business specializing in labor recruitment and placement services. Founded in 2018 in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, it is officially authorized by the Ministry of Labor & Skills. Our organization is backed by a skilled and dedicated team, committed to overseeing and streamlining the recruitment process for our clients. We focus on fostering long-term relationships by connecting employers with qualified professionals, creating partnerships that benefit both sides. Discover the potential of strategic workforce solutions with MRH, where expertise meets opportunity, and collaborations flourish.
